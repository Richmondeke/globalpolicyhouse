The Global Policy House (GPH) exists to close the digital, finance and identity divide ensuring that no one is left behind. GPH is the gateway to Africa, driving the Digital Revolution and linking the world to the African Market of over 1.3 billion people with a GDP of over US$3.5 trillion.
